Noodles Recipe

Noodles are a staple food in many cultures made from unleavened dough which is stretched, extruded, or rolled flat and cut into one of a variety of shapes. While long, thin strips may be the most common, many varieties of noodles are cut into waves, helices, tubes, strings, or shells, or folded over, or cut into other shapes. Noodles are usually cooked in boiling water, sometimes with cooking oil or salt added.

Ingredients Required

  • Noodles (Any)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Vegetables (If you like any)
  • Protien(chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.)
  • Sauce(Soy/Normal)
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Green Onion
  • Chilli Paste(If u want)

Process Step

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and protein of your choice. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender and protein is cooked through.
  4. Add cooked noodles to the skillet or wok, along with soy sauce and any other seasonings desired. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions and optional chili paste or sauce.
